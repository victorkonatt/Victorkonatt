What is emotional intelligence?


Emotional intelligence is ability to identify and manage self and others emotions through emotional awareness, body language and other emotional behavior
How is it different to IQ?
IQ only involves baseline knowledge like mathematics, visualizing objects in space and working memory questions to determine anyone intelligence.
