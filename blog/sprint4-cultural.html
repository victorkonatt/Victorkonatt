How understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people? & how might you engage with the principals of neuroplasticity for your own benefit?



Neuroplasticity principles help us understand that anyone can learn anything. Before neuroplasticity study researchers believed that brains stops growing after certain age which kind of stops people from learning new things. Our brain is like plastic that is why it’s called neuroplasticity. Every time we think or do a new task a new road map is created in the brain. And the more time we repeat the new task stronger the connection between the roads. Hence the principles give people a hope that they can never give up.


Sometimes when doing the course I have been facing a lot of times where I think I don’t know if I can do the exercises or not but understanding the principals and be positive not only doing this course but learning anything in future.
